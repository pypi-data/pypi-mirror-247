Note: Before using the keyboard you must set the focus within the frame by clicking once on the frame.

KeyAction
Status (shift-click to update multiple)
1-5 Set status(es) to 1 (Learning) to 5 (Learned)
i Mark term(s) as "Ignored"
w Mark term(s) as "Well Known"
Navigation
Right Move to next word
Shift + Right Move to next unknown or learning word (skip known and ignored)
Left Move to previous word
Shift + Left Move to previous unknown or learning word (skip known and ignored)
Misc
t
Shift + t		 Translate the sentence or paragraph of the current word
c
Shift + c		 Copy the sentence or whole paragraph of the current word to clipboard
Appearance
m Switch to the next theme
h Toggle term highlighting
